The Digitalcoin Foundation Needs YOU

The newly re-formed Digitalcoin Foundation is on a mission to take the Digitalcoin project to the next level. Contributions of your time, effort, expertise, and money, are the building blocks, and your opinions direct the construction.

Whether you have just learned about digital currencies, or you have been involved in the industry for years, YOU can make a difference. Represent your country as a core member, or strengthen the organization as an associate member, or just integrate Digitalcoin into your project, and help us make history.

At Your Service

Core & Associate

The Digitalcoin Foundation consists of core members and associate members. Right now it's one core member per country, and no limit on associate members.

Dues & Contributions

100% of annual member dues and contributions goes toward development, education, and marketing, with complete transparency into all spending.

Core Members

Core members set the standards, policies, and direction

Associate Members

Associate members have voting rights on key issues, and are given first opportunity to accept Foundation sponsored project work.
